Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about setting up your branded ordering website.

How does the delivery network work? +
When an order lands on your website, our system immediately sends the details to our delivery partner network. A nearby courier is auto-assigned, accepts the delivery route, and arrives at your restaurant just as the food finished cooking. It is completely automated and hands-off.
Who pays the delivery partner drivers? +
Your guests pay a standard, local distance-based delivery fee during checkout, just like they do on third-party aggregators. That fee goes directly to cover the partner dispatch cost. You do not pay anything out of pocket.
Do I have to build the website myself? +
No. We do 100% of the build and configuration for you. Send us your logo, menu list, and delivery boundaries, and we'll handle domain mapping, Stripe links, menu uploads, and SEO parameters. You'll be taking orders in under 48 hours.
Can Kitchio run alongside third-party apps? +
Absolutely. Kitchio is designed to sit comfortably alongside your existing aggregators. Most of our partners run Kitchio on their custom domain while slowly migrating high-value local regulars off third-party platforms to save on high fee commissions.
